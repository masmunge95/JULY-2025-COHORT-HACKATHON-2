How do I upgrade my account?

You can upgrade your account by visiting the Upgrade page and choosing a plan that suits you.

What are the limitations of the free plan?

Free users are limited to 10 tasks per month and 3 tasks per day. Access is restricted to basic quizzes, flashcards, and explanations in Math, Science, and English.
